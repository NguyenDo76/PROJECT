	OVERVIEW
The dataset is the information of sales of retail company. Based on available information about revenue in many different regions.
Using pre-process and explore data (EDA), time series method and visualization with Python.
Thereby solving the set goal that select the product with the highest revenue in the region and top 1 in revenue and 
predict the business situation of this product in the next 17 weeks.

	INSIGHT
Select the product with the highest revenue in the region and top 1 in revenue and predict the business situation of this product in the next 17 weeks.

	WORKFLOW
- Learn about the company, mission, business model, thereby gaining insight.
- Survey and understand data.
- Cleaning and EDA data: drop redundant columns and null values
- Visualize and analysis: using time series method and visualization with Python to analysis
- Build model and forecasting
